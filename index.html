About Commonplace

Commonplace is a publication of Ecotrust focused on stories of home. Our hope is to create places for connection, for mapping out new understandings of what is common across states, nations, and human nature. We look forward to hearing your stories about the places you live, your experiences of the stories we publish, and your ideas about how to evolve Commonplace in future issues. Share your thoughts on Facebook, Twitter, or write to us at info@commonplacemagazine.org

Acknowledgements

We extend our gratitude first and foremost to the people of the Skeena, who shared their stories and homes in contribution to this project. And there are many others that we would like to give our heartfelt thanks:

To The Charles Engelhard Foundation, Faraway Foundation, Thendara Foundation, Deborah J. and Peter A. Magowan Family Foundation, Neukom Family Foundation, and The Tim and Karen Hixon Foundation whose generous contributions made this project possible.

To our friends at Ecotrust Canada and Skeena Wild Conservation Trust, who are working on the ground in Skeena Country at the intersection of conservation and community economic development.
